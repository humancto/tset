TinyShakespeare corpus
======================

The text content is drawn from Shakespeare's plays, which are in the public
domain. This particular concatenation was distributed by Andrej Karpathy as
part of the char-rnn project:

  https://github.com/karpathy/char-rnn

The TSET project redistributes the file unmodified for use as a showcase
dataset under CC0 1.0 Universal:

  https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

No warranty.
